Hi, I am a master student in the Department of EE at Tsinghua University.
I work in Speech Processing and Machine Intelligence (SPMI) lab, advised by Professor Zhijian Ou.
I received my B.Sc degree in Department of Physics from Tsinghua University in 2016.

My main research interests are natural language processing and machine learning.
I am now working on spoken dialog systems.
